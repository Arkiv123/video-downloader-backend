Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

We run a zero-log tool. We do not create accounts, we do not store the links you paste, we do not keep a history of what you download, and we do not sell data to anyone. The media you download goes from the source to your device.

1. What we don't collect

By design, GOOGLY RANKS does not require you to sign up or log in, and the service does not persist:

  • Your name, email, or any account identity (there are no accounts).
  • A stored history of the URLs you submit or the media you download.
  • Your IP address in any long-lived log tied to your activity.

2. What is processed transiently

To fulfill a single request, a link you submit is sent to our backend so it can read the source's public metadata and return the available formats. This processing is transient and is not written to a durable user-activity log. Where technically possible, downloads are handed off so that media flows directly from the source's servers to your device.

3. Hosting and infrastructure

The Service is served through Cloudflare (frontend delivery and security) and runs its backend on a third-party cloud host. These providers may process limited technical request data (such as IP addresses) transiently for security, abuse-prevention, and delivery purposes, governed by their own privacy policies. We do not combine that data into user profiles.

4. Cookies and analytics

The core tool functions without tracking cookies. If analytics or advertising partners are added in the future, this policy will be updated to describe them, and any such use will be limited to aggregate, non-identifying measurement wherever possible.

5. Affiliate links

Some links on the Service may be affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. These partners set their own cookies and privacy terms on their own sites; we do not receive your payment details.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

7. Your choices

Because we do not build user profiles or store activity history, there is generally no personal dataset for us to export or delete. If you have a privacy question, contact us below.

8. Contact
